The Mission » To build simple and powerful software for boaters

Passage Planner beta » The first

Passage Planner came out of my travels up and down the East Coast of North America, from the Florida Keys to Nova Scotia. I did this piecemeal, while working 40+ hours a week remotely, so I had to plan a lot of passages. Every passage involved calculating travel times, looking up tides, currents, daylight hours, wind and wave predictions. I decided to automate it.

Check it out!
